# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to OctoAcme's project management documentation hub. This collection of guides provides a comprehensive framework for delivering high-quality projects efficiently while maintaining transparency, collaboration, and continuous improvement across teams.

OctoAcme's project management approach is built on five core principles: customer-first prioritization, iterative delivery of small testable increments, clear ownership with named Project and Product Managers, data-informed decision making, and psychological safety that encourages feedback and learning. Our projects follow a structured lifecycle from initiation through planning, execution, release, and retrospective, with each phase supported by specific artifacts and checkpoints. This framework applies to all cross-functional projects that deliver product features, services, or integrations.

Our delivery model emphasizes consistent team rhythm and communication cadence. Teams conduct daily standups, twice-weekly delivery syncs, and regular demos to maintain momentum and alignment. Project Managers coordinate delivery schedules and risk management, Product Managers define outcomes and prioritize the backlog, and Developers collaborate on design and implementation. Quality is built in through comprehensive testing at unit, integration, and end-to-end levels, with security scanning integrated into CI pipelines. We maintain transparency through project boards, risk registers, and regular stakeholder updates.

Continuous improvement is central to our culture. After each sprint, release, or milestone, teams conduct retrospectives to capture learnings and define actionable improvements. We track success metrics defined in project one-pagers, measure velocity and burndown, and use dashboards to monitor key signals. This documentation serves as the foundation for onboarding new team members, aligning stakeholders, and ensuring consistent project execution across OctoAcme.
